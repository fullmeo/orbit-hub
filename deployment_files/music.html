2018

S-Moi

S-Moi is Allyson's debut album, introducing her distinctive reggae-pop sound. Released in 2018, this album establishes the foundation for her artistic voice, blending Caribbean rhythms with contemporary soul and jazz elements.

Musical Style: Reggae-pop foundation with soul, jazz, and Caribbean influences. Rich orchestration and authentic storytelling throughout.
