About Allyson

Reggae-Pop Artist | Soul & Jazz Fusion | Paris-based Musician

Artist Biography

Allyson Glado is a reggae-pop artist based in Paris who seamlessly blends soul, jazz, and Caribbean influences into a unique sonic experience. With two albums — S-Moi (2018) and Élévation (2021) — she has established herself as an innovative voice in contemporary music.

Her music explores themes of personal growth, cultural fusion, and artistic authenticity. Collaborating with world-class musicians like trumpeter Serigne Diagne, Allyson creates rich, layered compositions that resonate with listeners across genres and cultures.

Rooted in reggae traditions but unafraid to experiment, Allyson's sound represents a bridge between Caribbean heritage and European sensibilities, creating music that feels both timeless and contemporary.

Musical Style & Influences

🎵 Reggae-Pop Foundation

Authentic Caribbean rhythms form the backbone of Allyson's music. Drawing inspiration from reggae pioneers while maintaining a contemporary pop sensibility.

🎺 Jazz Fusion Elements

Complex harmonies, improvisation, and sophisticated arrangements courtesy of collaborations with jazz musicians like Serigne Diagne.

🎤 Soul & R&B Influences

Soulful vocal delivery and emotional depth, combined with modern R&B production techniques and groovy bass lines.

🌴 Authentic Production

Real instruments, organic arrangements, and thoughtful production that prioritizes musicianship over trend-chasing.

Notable Collaborations

Serigne Diagne

Trumpet Virtuoso

Featured across Allyson's albums, Serigne Diagne brings international experience and sophisticated jazz sensibilities. A musician with 40+ years of classical training and collaborations with legends like Youssou Ndour and Michael Bublé.
