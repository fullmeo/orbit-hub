Allyson Glado

Reggae-Pop Authenticity. Deep Lyricism. Unbridled Energy.

Allyson Glado creates original reggae-pop sonorities that blend authentic reggae grooves, sensible pop melodies, and profound lyricism. Based in Paris and collaborating with trumpeter Serigne Diagne, she crafts music designed to help audiences relax while embracing their authentic selves.

Artistic Philosophy

  • Authenticity + Truth: Music rooted in genuine expression, never manufactured
  • Sensuality + Freedom: Celebrating the full spectrum of human emotion
  • Taboo-Breaking: Unafraid to address what others leave unsaid
  • Emotional Depth: Lyrics that resonate beyond the surface
  • Energy + Passion: Grooves that move body and soul

Musical Influences

Reggae — Caribbean roots, conscious messaging
Pop — Modern accessibility, contemporary production
Soul — Emotional vulnerability and vocal power
Jazz — Harmonic complexity, collaborative improvisation
Urban — Current cultural energy and contemporary voice

Collaboration with Serigne Diagne

Working with trumpeter and arranger Serigne Diagne, Allyson Glado elevates reggae-pop into sophisticated orchestral territory. His trumpet work, arrangements, and artistic direction transform reggae grooves into richly textured compositions that honor Caribbean traditions while pushing contemporary boundaries.

Artistic Partnership: Serigne Diagne

Serigne Diagne, accomplished trumpeter and arranger, brings orchestral sophistication to Allyson Glado's reggae-pop vision. His contributions extend beyond musicianship—he serves as artistic director, shaping the sonic identity of her work.

What Serigne Brings

  • Trumpet Arrangements: Distinctive horn work that elevates reggae grooves
  • Harmonic Complexity: Jazz-influenced chord progressions and musical arrangements
  • Artistic Direction: Strategic vision for sonic evolution and album coherence
  • Collaborative Spirit: Deep creative partnership respecting Allyson's artistic voice
