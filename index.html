About Allyson Glado

Allyson Glado is a reggae-pop artist who seamlessly blends soul, jazz, and reggae influences into a unique sonic experience. With albums S-Moi (2018) and Élévation (2021), she has established herself as an innovative voice in contemporary music.

Her music explores themes of personal growth, cultural fusion, and artistic authenticity. Collaborating with musicians like trumpeter Serigne Diagne, Allyson creates rich, layered compositions that resonate with listeners across genres.
