📀 ÉLÉVATION

Élévation

2021

A Journey of Artistic Growth

Élévation is Allyson Glado's second studio album, representing a maturation of her reggae-pop sound. Blending soul, jazz, and reggae influences, this album showcases her artistic evolution and collaboration with musician Serigne Diagne. Each track tells a story of personal and musical elevation.

📀 S-MOI

S-Moi

2018

The Debut Album

S-Moi marks Allyson Glado's debut studio album, introducing the world to her unique reggae-pop fusion sound. This album establishes her artistic voice, blending Caribbean rhythms with soul and jazz elements. With soulful vocals and rich production, S-Moi laid the foundation for her artistic journey.
